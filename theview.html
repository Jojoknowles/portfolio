The View opening, 2024

6 december, The View, The Hague.


Opening of my studio/artist-run initiative/antikraak space.

I invited a group of artists to exhibit in the small basement space accessible by a hole in the floor of the studio, the artists were chosen based on how well their works would suit being in a basement.
