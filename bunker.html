Sleep gunk, 2023

    12-25 june, Waterkant bunker, Westbroekpark, The Hague.

    Solo exhibition in the Waterkant bunker for the collective/exhibition series Safe Space for Stupid Ideas; curated by Maarten Keus, Sonya Umanskaya and Mila Narjollet.

    Selected works were chosen and installed by the artist, in 4 rooms plus hallways in the damp, cold and dark bunker.
